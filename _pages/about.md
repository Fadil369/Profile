I'm a physician and healthcare AI architect with over 10 years of experience spanning clinical practice, healthcare AI innovation, and interoperability systems. As the Founder and CEO of [BrainSAIT](https://www.brainsait.io), I lead the development of AI solutions that transform healthcare delivery.

## Mission

To decode the complexity of healthcare through **intelligent automation**, **interoperability**, and **human-centric AI**. My work bridges the gap between the chaotic reality of clinical practice and the precision of modern data science. I believe in the concept of **Original Intelligence (OI) + Artificial Intelligence (AI)** ‚Äî combining human clinical judgment with advanced AI capabilities to enhance patient care and streamline healthcare operations.
