## Professional Summary

Founder and CEO of BrainSAIT, creator of published PyPI packages (brainsait-pyheart & brainsait-pybrain), and expert in FHIR/HL7 standards, Clinical NLP, and healthcare interoperability.

- **Medical Coding & RCM**: AI-powered ICD-10/CPT coding, claims automation, and revenue cycle optimization.
